About Us

Shyam Ji Pede Wale (श्याम जी पेड़े वाले) is a traditional sweet shop located in Dantaramgarh, Sikar. The shop is situated around 20 km from Khatu Shyam Ji Temple and is known for fresh handmade pede and sweets for prasad and special occasions.

Prasad Sweets for Khatu Shyam Ji

If you are looking for a prasad shop near Khatu Shyam Ji, we offer fresh pede, laddu and sweets. Our shop is a popular stop for devotees traveling to the temple.
