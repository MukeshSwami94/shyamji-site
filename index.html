About Us

Welcome to Shyam Ji Pede Wale (श्याम जी पेड़े वाले) — a place where tradition, purity, and authentic Rajasthani taste come together. Located in Dantaramgarh, Sikar, around 20 km from the famous Khatu Shyam Ji Temple, our shop has been serving fresh handmade sweets and traditional prasad since 1985.

We are known for our soft and delicious handmade pede, prepared using traditional methods and pure ingredients to maintain the authentic desi taste of Rajasthan. Every sweet is crafted with care, freshness, and devotion, making it perfect for prasad, festivals, weddings, family celebrations, and special occasions.

Our shop offers a variety of traditional sweets and namkeen including handmade pede, milk cake, barfi, besan barfi, laddu, gulab jamun, besan namkeen, and mixture namkeen. We also take custom and bulk orders for religious functions, bhog, festivals, and celebrations.

With years of trust and customer love, Shyam Ji Pede Wale has become a popular destination for devotees and sweet lovers visiting near Khatu Shyam Ji Temple. We warmly welcome you to experience the royal traditional taste of Rajasthan.

Prasad Sweets for Khatu Shyam Ji

If you are looking for a prasad shop near Khatu Shyam Ji, we offer fresh pede, laddu and sweets. Our shop is a popular stop for devotees traveling to the temple.
